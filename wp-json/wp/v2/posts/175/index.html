1. From top to bottom<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Each task is considered from the most superficial introductions, gradually clarifying each aspect down to the smallest details.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

2. Honesty<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Honesty is crucial for me as it builds trust, ensures clarity, and fosters genuine connections with users, leading to more meaningful experiences.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

3. No design is good design<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Using fewer elements effectively to accomplish most tasks, making it easier for people to understand and engage with, simplifies processes<\/p>\n\n\n\n

4. Design is team effort\u2019s outcome<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Emphasizes collaboration, showcasing how collective contributions shape the final product, ensuring diverse perspectives and comprehensive solutions.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

5. Aesthetics always come last<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Aesthetics are prioritized last in the design process, ensuring functionality and usability precede visual appeal for a more effective end result.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
