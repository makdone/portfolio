Design principles

February 2, 2023

1. From top to bottom

Each task is considered from the most superficial introductions, gradually clarifying each aspect down to the smallest details.

2. Honesty

Honesty is crucial for me as it builds trust, ensures clarity, and fosters genuine connections with users, leading to more meaningful experiences.

3. No design is good design

Using fewer elements effectively to accomplish most tasks, making it easier for people to understand and engage with, simplifies processes

4. Design is team effort’s outcome

Emphasizes collaboration, showcasing how collective contributions shape the final product, ensuring diverse perspectives and comprehensive solutions.

5. Aesthetics always come last

Aesthetics are prioritized last in the design process, ensuring functionality and usability precede visual appeal for a more effective end result.
